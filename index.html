I am an Assistant Professor of Electronic and Computer Engineering at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. Before joining HKUST, I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Stanford University, working with Prof. Lei Xing.

My goal is to develop machine learning tools that are robust, reliable and ready for real-world clinical applications. Specifically, my research has been focused on two broad themes: developing a precise understanding of the functioning of widely-used deep learning techniques; and avenues to make machine learning methods robust and secure from an adversarial viewpoint. .

News

  • I'm honored to have received the ACM Doctoral Dissertation Award Honorable Mention, the Joint AAAI/ACM SIGAI Doctoral Dissertation Award, and the MIT George M. Sprowls PhD Thesis Award in Artificial Intelligence and Decision-Making.
